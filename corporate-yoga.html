Corporate Yoga

Corporate & Executive Yoga Classes To Relieve Stress And Enhance Productivity At Work

Remote Classes — Online Streamed Sessions

These classes are ideal for companies who want to keep their employees healthy and productive while also providing a sense of community in this challenging economic and social landscape. Classes will be conducted via Zoom. All you need to participate is a camera and a mic.

Melbourne Corporate Yoga and Meditation Classes

These classes can be tailored to any level and need, from simple stretching sessions to yoga and meditation. There is no max number of attendees for these sessions and it’s a great way to give back to your staff.
